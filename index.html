FAQ

Can I bring a plus-one?

We promise this is nothing personal! Due to the intimate nature of our celebration and the limited space at our venue, we're only able to accommodate those guests formally invited. You'll find the names of invited guests printed on your invitation. We hope you understand, and we can't wait to celebrate with you!

Can I bring my child?

We love your little ones! However, to ensure everyone's safety and comfort, we've decided to make this an adults-only celebration. Our venue features an outdoor setting with open flames, alcohol, and other elements that are best suited for guests 18 and over. We hope this allows parents to relax and enjoy an evening out! Thank you for understanding.

What should I wear?

This is a semi-formal event. No idea what that means? Check out this link. Basically, dress nice but comfortably. Here are some pointers:

  • Skip the Stilettos: This is an outdoor wedding! If you wear thin heels, you'll be stuck in the grass and it'll ruin your pretty shoes. Nobody wants that.
  • Layer Up: The wedding will take place outside in late September in Ontario. Check the weather, wear what will be comfy, and bring extra layers if you need! If it's cold, we will have heating and blankets available, but we don't want anyone to freeze.
  • Wear What Makes You Confident: Seriously. This is an intimate, welcoming family event. Be yourself, be comfy, and celebrate with us!
Will the ceremony/reception be indoors or outdoors?

Both will be outdoors, but we will have bathrooms indoors!

What time should I arrive?

Wedding starts at 5pm SHARP. A Virgo is getting married. When I say sharp, I mean SHARP. We recommend guests start arriving around 4pm so you can get comfortable and acquainted with the space. Those in the wedding party will have their schedule sent to them at least two weeks prior to the event.

Will there be food/drinks?

No, we're going to let you starve. Of course! We will have plenty of food and drinks available! However, please note there will NOT be an open bar. We will be providing some beverages, and of course, there will be water available at all times. Feel free to bring a couple beers or a bottle of wine to split with friends and family if you would like!

What if I have dietary restrictions?

Please let us know in the RSVP form! We will do our best to accommodate guests, but please know that we are preparing all the food ourselves and cannot guarantee anything is allergy safe. If you have food allergies, please mention that in the RSVP form and send Caitlin or Karla an email. For guests with food allergies, we strongly recommend bringing your own food to avoid any possible cross contamination.

What's the weather like in September?

Honestly, it's hit or miss. We recommend checking the weather one week before the wedding to get the most accurate forecast!
